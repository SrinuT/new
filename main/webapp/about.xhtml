About Us

iHealthMantra is connecting customers and healthcare providers in a way never done before. Our objective is to transform the way customers connect with healthcare providers by creating a win-win situation. For customers it means more choice, informed decisions and better savings. For healthcare providers it means wider reach, better customer understanding and higher ROI on marketing spend.

Leadership Team

Dr. Prajakta Sawwalakhe -Founder & CEO

Dr. Prajakta Sawwalakhe is a Founder and Chief Executive Officer of iHealthMantra. Dr. Sawwalakhe is passionate about bringing world class healthcare services to patients by connecting healthcare providers and patients.



Dr. Sawwalakhe is a certified dermatologist, a fellow of American Academy of professional medicine and has 10 years of global healthcare experience. She is associated with El-Camino Hospital Mountain view California , UPSC Pittsburgh PA and St Anthony Hospital Oklahoma. She has also been involved in clinical research at Stanford University.
